### If Questions Come Up

**Q: Why not use USDC?**
A: USDC can't execute conditional logic. You can't say "pay only if success" with USDC - it's just transfers. MNEE's programmability enables the rules and automation we need.

**Q: Is this really autonomous?**
A: Yes - once rules are set, payments execute automatically. No human in the loop. The agent operates as an independent economic entity.

**Q: What about security?**
A: All transactions are on-chain, transparent, and auditable. Spending limits, approvals, and rules are enforced by smart contracts.

**Q: Can agents earn money?**
A: Yes - agents can receive payments directly to their treasury. The deposit we showed is one example, but agents can receive revenue from completing tasks, providing services, etc.
